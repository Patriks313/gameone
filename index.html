How to play

The setup
The host creates a room and shares a 4-digit code. Everyone joins on their own phone. When the host starts, two players are secretly chosen as Evil.
The evil pair
Evil players receive a secret password — one word each from a pair. They know both words and who carries which, and must find each other through conversation without being caught.
The good players
Good players only know they are not evil. Listen carefully — the evil pair will try to slip their passwords into natural conversation to signal each other.
Voting
When the host opens voting, everyone taps who they suspect on their own phone. The host closes voting and everyone sees the result simultaneously.
Winning
Good wins when both evil players are eliminated. Evil wins when evil players equal or outnumber the good players remaining.
