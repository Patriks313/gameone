How to play

The setup
The host creates a room and shares a 4-digit code. Everyone joins on their own phone. Two players are secretly chosen as Evil.
The evil pair
Evil players each receive a secret word from a related pair. They must find each other through conversation without being caught.
The good players
Good players only know they are not evil. Listen for the hidden words slipping into conversation.
Voting
The host opens voting. Everyone taps their suspect. Voting closes automatically when all have voted, or the host can force-close it.
Winning
Good wins when both evil players are eliminated. Keep voting until they are found.
