How to play

The setup
Shadow Council is designed as a side activity — something to play alongside a dinner, a trip, or any gathering. The host creates a room and shares a 4-digit code. Everyone joins on their own phone. Two players are secretly chosen as Evil.
The evil pair
Evil players each receive a secret word from a related pair. Their aim is to find each other through conversation — and eliminate the good players before being caught themselves.
The good players
Good players only know they are not evil. Their aim is to identify and eliminate both evil players. Listen carefully — the evil pair will try to slip their secret words into natural conversation to signal each other.
Voting
The host opens voting. Everyone taps their suspect. The player with the most votes is eliminated each round — in case of a tie, all tied players are eliminated. Voting closes automatically when all have voted, or the host can force-close it. In the final round, eliminated players also get to vote.
Winning
Good wins when both evil players are eliminated. Evil wins if the evil players outlast all the good ones. Keep voting until the game is decided.
