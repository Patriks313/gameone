Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · greblats.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Shadow Council ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Patrik Stalbarg via greblats.com, handles information when you use our game.

1. Information we collect

Shadow Council is designed to collect as little information as possible. When you play:

Player names — you choose a name to join a game. This name is stored temporarily in our database (Firebase, operated by Google) only for the duration of your game session and is not linked to any personal identity.

Game data — votes, roles, and game state are stored temporarily in Firebase while a game is active. This data is not associated with any account or personal information.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history and interests. This is managed entirely by Google.

For more information on how Google uses data in advertising, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting: google.com/settings/ads

3. Cookies

We do not set any cookies ourselves. Google AdSense and Firebase may set cookies as part of their services. These are governed by Google's privacy policy.

4. Third-party services

Firebase (Google) — we use Firebase Realtime Database to sync game state between players in real time. Firebase is operated by Google LLC. Data stored in Firebase is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Google AdSense — we use AdSense to display ads. AdSense is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Google Fonts — we use Google Fonts to display typography. Google may collect limited data when fonts are loaded. See Google's privacy policy.

5. Data retention

Game session data (player names, votes, roles) is stored in Firebase only while a game is active. We do not deliberately retain this data after a game ends, though Firebase may retain it briefly as part of normal database operations.

6. Children's privacy

Shadow Council is intended for players aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through our service, please contact us.

7. Your rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about data processed by Google services, please refer to Google's privacy tools at myaccount.google.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: privacy@greblats.com
